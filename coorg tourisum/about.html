CAMPING AND HIKING

Coorg is situated in the Western Ghats, which makes it a perfect location for hiking and camping due to its scenic mountain ranges and diverse flora and fauna.

WILDLIFE

Coorg is a wildlife destination in India that is home to various species such as elephants, tigers, and deer. Nagarhole National Park and Brahmagiri Wildlife Sanctuary are the popular reserves in the region.

MOUNTAIN BIKING

Mountain biking in Coorg involves riding bicycles off-road on rough terrains such as mountains, hills, and forests. Popular trails include routes through coffee plantations and wildlife sanctuaries.

CAMPING

Coorg is a popular camping destination in southern India, known for its stunning natural beauty, trekking trails, and serene environment. Camping in Coorg offers a great opportunity to experience the region's pristine wilderness and enjoy a peaceful break from city life.

Amazing COORG.

Coorg, also known as Kodagu, is a popular tourist destination located in the western ghats of Karnataka, India. It is known for its picturesque landscapes, misty hills, coffee plantations, waterfalls, and wildlife. The region has a rich cultural heritage, with a significant population of the Kodava people who have their own unique customs and traditions. Coorg is also famous for its delicious cuisine, including traditional dishes such as Pandi Curry, Noolputtu, and Kachampuli. With its pleasant weather throughout the year, Coorg is a perfect destination for nature lovers, adventure enthusiasts, and those seeking a peaceful getaway.
