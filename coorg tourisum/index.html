Travel blogs

Coorg, also known as Kodagu, is a beautiful hill station located in the southern Indian state of Karnataka. Known for its lush greenery, coffee plantations, and breathtaking views, Coorg is a popular destination among nature lovers and adventure enthusiasts. The town is surrounded by misty hills, dense forests, and meandering rivers, making it a perfect getaway for those seeking peace and serenity. Coorg is also famous for its unique culture and cuisine, with traditional dishes like pork curry and bamboo shoot pickle being some of the local favorites. Whether you're looking for a peaceful retreat or an adrenaline-filled adventure, Coorg has something for everyone.
